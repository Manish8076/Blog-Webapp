Who we are


IIMT Greater Noida Highlights 2023 Accordion Icon V3 IIMT Noida was established in 1994 and is approved by AICTE, the Pharmacy Council of India, and the Bar Council of India. IIMT was established under the aegis of the Association of Management Studies and is accredited by NAAC and NBA. IIMT Greater Noida, through more than seven colleges, offers UG, PG, and other courses to students across Engineering, Computer Science, and various other streams. Some of the colleges under the IIMT Group of Colleges are affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, the Board of Technical Education, Uttar Pradesh, and Chaudhary Charan Singh University. IIMT Group of Colleges is ranked 144 by The Times 2022 for BTech.
